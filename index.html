About IDR

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led training and deployment institute designed to address the critical shortage of skilled digital, cyber, and technology risk professionals in regulated and high-consequence environments.

Through our exclusive UK university partnerships, we bridge the gap between cutting-edge academic insight and practical industry needs. We focus on developing practitioners who are equipped to navigate the complexities of AI governance, cybersecurity resilience, and modern digital risks.
